Version 1.7.0: 30 new elements, JSON save/load, 60+ new reactions

  NEW ELEMENTS (v1.7.0):
    Powders:  Charcoal, Sulfur, Coal, Chalk, Gravel, Rust
    Solids:   Steel, Diamond, Wax, Ceramic, Brick, Crystal
    Liquids:  Alcohol, Mercury, Honey, Tar, Slime, Blood
    Gasses:   Hydrogen, Oxygen, Nitrogen, Propane, Fog
    Extras:   Lightning, Antimatter, Ember, Gel, Void, NitroGel, Superacid

  NEW REACTIONS (v1.7.0):
    Acid + Steam            = Toxic Gas
    Acid + Alcohol          = Explosion
    Superacid + Steam       = Toxic Gas
    Superacid + Lava        = Plasma burst
    Superacid dissolves:    Rubber, Glass, Obsidian, Steel, Crystal, Brick, Ceramic, Diamond (slowly)
    Water + Sulfur          = Acid
    Water + Salt            = Saltwater  (existing, now also produces Crystal on evap)
    Water + Nitrogen        = Ice
    Saltwater + Cooler      = Ice
    Saltwater evap          = Steam + Salt or Crystal (random)
    Steam + Cooler          = Fog
    Steam + Acid            = Toxic Gas
    Fog + Heat              = Steam
    Fog + Cold/Nitrogen/Ice = Water
    Fog + Acid              = Toxic Gas
    Sand + Heater/Lava/Coal = Glass
    Wood burn               = Fire + Charcoal (nearby)
    Mud + sustained heat    = Brick
    Mud + brief heat        = Dirt
    Oil + extreme heat      = Tar
    Ice + Lava              = Obsidian + Steam
    Snow/Ice + Lava         = Water
    Lava + Nitrogen         = Stone
    Stone + Superacid       = destroyed
    Charcoal (burning)      → Ember + Smoke → Ash
    Coal (burning)          → heats neighbors, melts water, emts smoke → Ash
    Sulfur + Fire/Lava      = Toxic Gas
    Wax + Fire/Lava         = Oil
    Steel + Water/Saltwater = Rust (over time)
    Steel + Heat            = conducts heat to neighbors
    Rust + Steel + Water    = spreads Rust
    Mercury + Heat          = Toxic Gas
    Mercury                 sinks through Water/Alcohol/Blood
    Alcohol + Fire          = burst of Fire + Steam
    Alcohol + Water         = Water (dilution)
    Alcohol + Acid          = Explosion
    Honey                   very slow flow, burns to fire
    Tar + Fire              = Fire + lots of Smoke
    Slime + Water           = Slime spreads
    Slime + Fire            = destroyed
    Hydrogen + Fire (+ O₂)  = huge explosion; near Oxygen produces Water
    Oxygen + Fire           = fire spreads faster
    Nitrogen + Fire         = extinguished
    Nitrogen + Water/Saltw  = Ice
    Nitrogen + Lava         = Stone
    Propane + Fire/Spark    = Explosion (sinks as heavy gas)
    Lightning               destroys path, ignites everything, triggers Fuse/Gunpowder/TNT/Methane/Bomb
    Ember + Water           = extinguished
    Ember dies              → puff of Smoke
    Gel                     absorbs Water/Blood, very slow flow
    NitroGel + nearly anything = massive explosion
    Void                    spreads, consumes all, then dissipates
    Antimatter              destroys everything including fire-immune materials
    Crystal                 grows in Saltwater
    Blood + Water           = dilutes to Water
    Blood + Fire            = Smoke
    Sponge                  now also absorbs Blood and Slime
    Virus                   now killed by Fire (existing) + Void
